How to Use :

Download the plugin from codecanyon.net and upload it through your plugins page.
Activate the plugin from your Wordpress plugins page.
Woocommerce Gift Card plugin allows you to sell redeemable gift cards on your Woocommerce store. The gift cards are coupon codes and can be applied to carts or products.
Go to add products and scroll to product data settings and check the product as "gift".

Creating the page for Gift card products

Use the shortcode [coupon_page] to output the gift products.
The gift products are then ouputed on the page in the front-end using the default woocommerce style sheet.

Setting Up Email Tepmlate

Navigate to the Woocommerce settings and click on "Gift Card" tab.
